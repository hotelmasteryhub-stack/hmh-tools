HOTEL MASTERY HUB
Housekeeping Daily Operations
Everything your HSK team needs day to day, in one place — room assignment, cleaning checklists, staff productivity, and shift history.
WHAT'S IN THIS BUNDLE
1
Setup — define your rooms, staff, and cleaning types once
Cleaning types, room types, the time matrix between them, your room list, task list, and staff roster. Update whenever something changes — otherwise, set once.
2
Room Board — today's workload at a glance
Every active room, its selected service, priority, workload, and assignment. See instantly if you're over or under staff capacity for today.
3
Assignment Sheet — assign rooms and tasks to staff
Assign each active room and every additional work item to a staff member. Auto-assign balances workload automatically. Set today's shift times per person.
4
Checklists — prove the clean was done right
Checkout Clean, Stayover Service, and Inspection checklists. Tick items as you clean, archive every completed room to its own 90-day history.
5
Productivity — is your team efficient, not just busy
Enter expected vs actual hours per person and see Team Overhour % automatically. Save a month to its own History Archive with a trend chart.
6
Shift History — save today, export straight to Productivity
Save each day's shift from the Assignment Sheet. Every saved shift rolls up automatically — no retyping — into the Productivity tab's expected/actual hours whenever you're ready to close out the month.
EXPORTS — WHAT EACH BUTTON DOES
PDFPrint the current tab

Prints whichever tab (and sub-tab, for Checklists/Productivity) is currently open — never the whole bundle at once.

✉Email the current tab

Sends a plain-text summary of whichever tab is open. Current tab only, never a full dump of everything.

CSVDownload the full bundle as a spreadsheet

Rooms, staff, and Productivity data across the whole bundle in one file.

BACKUP ↓Save everything

Downloads one .json file with all data from every tab — Setup, Room Board, Checklists, Productivity, and Shift History.

RESTORE ↑Load a backup file

Reads a BACKUP ↓ file back in and restores every tab. Asks for confirmation before replacing anything.

ONE RULE

If it isn't in this tool, it isn't tracked. Assign every room, tick every checklist, save every shift. The record is what protects you when a guest disputes a room, or when you need to prove your team is performing.

Staff Data

One row per housekeeper currently in Setup. Diff % calculates automatically.

SHOWING
Expected Hrs is the standard target time for everything assigned that day (rooms + tasks + common areas, at their default minutes) — computed automatically, not typed in. Room Hrs is the real time, from each person's sign-off actual finish time on Assignment Sheet; until sign-off is filled in for a day, it falls back to the standard estimate. All of this builds up automatically as shifts are saved to history for the month/year set in Setup — no export needed. To add or remove staff, use Setup's Staff List; this table follows it automatically. Flag new staff as N and volunteers as V — their hours are tracked but excluded from the adjusted KPI so training drag does not skew your core team results.
