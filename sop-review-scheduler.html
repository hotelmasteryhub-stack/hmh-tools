Operations Manual Review Scheduler
Keep your operations manual alive. Monthly quick checks. Quarterly deep reviews. A change log so nothing gets lost.
WHAT THIS TOOL DOES

An operations manual that is never reviewed becomes irrelevant within months. This tool keeps your review rhythm on track — monthly quick checks to catch gaps from recent situations, quarterly deep reviews to check everything is still accurate.

Every review generates findings and actions. The change log captures what was updated and when. The update triggers list tells staff and managers exactly when the manual needs an immediate update — without waiting for the next scheduled review.

HOW TO USE — FOUR TABS
1
Monthly Review — quick monthly check
Five review items covering the most likely sources of gaps — recent mistakes, recurring questions, complaints, contact list accuracy, and new situations. For each: findings, actions taken, and a done checkbox. Add the review date and who conducted it.
2
Quarterly Review — deeper structural check
Six review items covering full process flows, training materials, seasonal changes, decision guidance accuracy, SOPs, and team feedback. Same structure: findings, actions, done.
3
Change Log — record every update
Every time the manual is updated, log it here — date, what section changed, what changed, and who made the change. A single source of truth for all changes.
4
Update Triggers — when to update immediately
A list of situations that require an immediate update — not waiting for the next review. Add your own triggers. Print this tab and post it where managers will see it.
