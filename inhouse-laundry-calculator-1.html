In-House Laundry Cost Calculator
Calculate what one room set actually costs to wash in-house. Compare to your outsourced rate. See if it makes financial sense.
WHAT THIS TOOL DOES

Enter your washing machine specs, utility rates, detergent cost, and staff time. The calculator works out your real in-house cost to wash one full room set, compares it to what your outsourced laundry supplier charges for the same weight, and tells you which is cheaper right now — plus how much you'd save per set, per month, and per year. If you're considering buying machines, the payback calculator at the bottom shows how long that investment would take to pay for itself.

HOW TO USE — THREE STEPS
1
Enter your machine and utility numbers
Machine capacity, kWh and litres per cycle come from the machine's spec label. Electricity and water rates come from your utility bills.
2
Enter detergent, staff time, and your room set
Weigh one full room set once (sheets, towels, bath mat) — that weight drives everything else. Add your outsourced supplier's rate per kg to compare against.
3
Read the verdict and check payback
The verdict box tells you which option is cheaper today. If you're weighing up buying machines, fill in the payback calculator to see how many years it would take to earn back the investment.

This calculation excludes machine depreciation. If machines are already owned and paid for, the saving shown here is your true day-to-day running cost advantage — not the full financial picture of owning machines from scratch.
