HSK Shift Planner PRO
Everything in the standard Shift Planner — plus 90-day shift history, weekly and monthly summaries, and one-click productivity export.
WHAT PRO ADDS OVER THE STANDARD VERSION
★
90-Day Shift History
Click SAVE TODAY TO HISTORY on the Assignment Sheet after each shift. The full snapshot is stored — rooms, staff, cleaning types, assignments, minutes. The last 90 days are always kept. Older entries roll off automatically. Download Full History exports everything before it rolls off.
★
Last 7 Days & Last 30 Days Summaries
The History tab shows team-level summaries for the last 7 and 30 days — total shifts logged, total rooms cleaned, average workload per shift, average overhour % per staff member across all shifts. Spot patterns before they become problems.
★
One-Click Productivity Export
Select a month. The tool reads all history entries for that month and calculates per-staff expected hours (sum of effective minutes) and actual hours (sum of assigned minutes). Click EXPORT TO PRODUCTIVITY TOOL — data loads directly into the HSK Productivity tool without re-entry.
STANDARD FEATURES — SAME AS SHIFT PLANNER
Configurable cleaning types — define your own: Departure, Stayover, Amenities Restock, Linen Change, etc.
Time matrix — cleaning type × room type. Suite departure = 50 min, dorm bed departure = 10 min.
Room-level tasks — add tasks per room card. Stacks on top of cleaning type time.
Per-staff shift length — each person has their own start/end time. Effective minutes per person.
Room board — individual room cards with real room numbers, auto-assign, floor and staff filters.
Printable assignment sheets — per staff member, with room numbers, cleaning type, tasks, target minutes, done ✓ column, sign-off line.
EXPORTS
PDF — prints the tab you are currently on only. Never prints all tabs.
✉ Email — sends a summary of the current tab only. History tab sends the 30-day summary.
DOWNLOAD THIS PAGE ↓ — same as PDF, at the bottom of each tab.
PRINT ALL / PRINT THIS — clean formatted assignment sheets with room numbers, done ✓, sign-off line.
DOWNLOAD FULL HISTORY — exports all archived shifts as CSV. Use before the 90-day rolling window removes old data.
EXPORT TO PRODUCTIVITY TOOL — writes selected month's data directly into HSK Productivity Tool localStorage. No re-entry needed.
CSV — exports room list, today's statuses, assignments, and capacity summary.
BACKUP ↓ — saves everything including full 90-day history as a .json file. Use when switching devices.
RESTORE ↑ — loads a backup. Confirms before replacing. Exported date shown.
ONE RULE

What you do not measure will surprise you. Save every shift to history. At the end of the month you will see exactly where the workload went, who was over capacity, and whether your cleaning targets are realistic. That is the data that changes decisions.
