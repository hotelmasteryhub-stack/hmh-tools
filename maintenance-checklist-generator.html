HOW TO USE
1
BUILD tab — select areas and customise items
Choose which hotel areas apply to your property. Add or remove items from daily, weekly, and monthly checklists. Add custom areas if needed.
2
YOUR CHECKLIST tab — use and tick off
Switch between Daily, Weekly, and Monthly view. Tick items as you complete them. Download the active checklist as a PDF reference card.
3
Download your checklist
Use DOWNLOAD THIS PAGE ↓ on the checklist tab to print the active frequency as a standalone reference card for your maintenance team.
