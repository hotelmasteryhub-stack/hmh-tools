HSK Group Stay Calculator
Calculate the full housekeeping cost for a group booking — labour, linen, and supplies. Know your break-even room rate before you quote.
WHAT THIS TOOL DOES

When a group books a block of rooms for a fixed stay, you need to know the total housekeeping cost before you set your rate. This tool calculates exactly that — labour for arrival cleans and stayover services, linen costs based on your actual bed set configuration, and supply costs for the stay.

Linen is calculated the right way — per bed set type (single, double, king) with the exact items or weight in each set. A family room with 2 single beds and 1 double is configured correctly and priced correctly.

The result: total HSK cost for the group, HSK cost per room per night, the minimum room rate to break even on housekeeping, and the rate needed to hit your target margin.

HOW TO USE — THREE STEPS
1
Setup — do once, update when rates change
Enter hotel name, gross hourly rate and employer multiplier. Choose your laundry pricing model — per piece or per kg. Define your bed set types with their items (per piece) or weight (per kg). Add room-level items like bath mats. Define each ROOM TYPE once — its departure and stayover clean times, and how many of each bed set / room item it uses. Enter monthly supply invoice and room nights sold to derive supply cost per night.
2
Calculator — enter the group booking details
Enter group name, check-in and check-out dates — stay length calculates automatically. The room list opens in read-only VIEW mode. Click ✎ EDIT to add a room row: pick a room type from your Setup catalog, then enter quantity, stayover cleans, and linen changes for this booking — cleaning times and linen composition come from the room type automatically.
3
Read the results and set your rate
Total HSK cost for the group, HSK per room per night, break-even room rate (covering HSK only), and rate with your target margin. Set your target margin % to see what room rate you need to quote.
IMPORTANT — WHAT THIS TOOL COVERS

This tool calculates housekeeping cost only. The break-even room rate shown is the minimum rate to cover HSK. Your actual room rate must also cover all other hotel costs — front desk, utilities, linen depreciation, overheads, and profit margin.

Use the break-even and target margin figures as a floor — never quote below the break-even rate unless you have a deliberate strategic reason.
