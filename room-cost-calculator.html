THE TOOL SUGGESTS A PRICE — NOT THE PRICE

The required price is based purely on your costs and target margin. It does not know what competitors charge or what the market will bear. Use it as a compass, not a ceiling. Selling below cost will erode your margin regardless of how full you are. Every extra room sold at any price above cost is better than an empty room.
