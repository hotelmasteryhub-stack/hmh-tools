WHAT THIS TOOL DOES

This tool tracks your actual HSK cost per room night sold every month. You enter your real monthly costs — wages per role, laundry invoice, supplies — and your room nights sold and ADR. The tool calculates your actual HSK cost per room night and breaks it down into wages, laundry, and supplies separately.

The 12-month history table shows the trend over time. A rising cost per room night means wages, laundry, or supplies changed — or room nights sold dropped. The trend tells you which direction you are moving before it becomes a problem.

You can optionally enter your design cost from the HSK Room Cost Calculator — the tool will then show how actual cost compares to what it should be by design every month.

HOW TO USE — MONTHLY WORKFLOW
1
First time — set up your wage roles
In the This Month tab, add each wage role — HSK Manager, Supervisor, Floor Housekeepers, Outsourced staff. Enter their total employer cost (not just gross wages). This structure saves and stays the same each month — you only update the amounts.
2
Each month — enter actual costs
Update month, year, room nights sold, and ADR. Enter actual wage costs per role from payroll. Enter the laundry invoice total. Enter monthly supply spend. All KPIs calculate automatically.
3
Save to history
Click SAVE TO HISTORY to record this month in the 12-month table. The history tab shows your trend — rising or falling HSK cost per room night over time.
4
Compare to design cost (optional)
Enter your design cost per room night from the HSK Room Cost Calculator. The tool shows the gap between what HSK costs by design and what it actually costs each month.
