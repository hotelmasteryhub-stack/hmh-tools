Processes tell people what normally happens. Decision guidance tells people what to do when reality changes.
WHAT THIS TOOL DOES

This tool builds your hotel's decision guidance library — what staff should do when situations fall outside the normal process. For each situation, you define the standard response, who handles it, and who to escalate to.

Each situation is marked with a severity level — L1 Low, L2 Medium, L3 High. L3 situations require immediate manager involvement. L1 situations staff can handle independently. Pre-filled with realistic hotel scenarios across six categories.

Add, edit, or remove situations freely. Each tab covers one department or phase. Download each tab separately as a clean reference card.

SEVERITY LEVELS
L1 LOW
Staff can handle independently
No manager involvement needed unless situation escalates. Document for review.
L2 MEDIUM
Inform manager — handle with guidance
Staff take first action but manager is informed. May need manager sign-off on resolution.
L3 HIGH
Manager involved immediately
Do not attempt to resolve alone. Contact manager immediately. Document everything.

ONE RULE

A process tells staff what to do when everything goes normally. Decision guidance tells them what to do when it doesn't. Most problems in hotels happen because the second part was never written down.
