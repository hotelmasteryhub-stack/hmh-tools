Guest Journey Mapper
Map how guests actually experience your hotel — Arrival, Stay, Departure. Your reality, not an ideal model.
WHAT THIS TOOL DOES

This tool helps you map the guest experience at your hotel across three phases — Arrival, Stay, and Departure. For each step in the journey you document what happens, who is responsible, and what can go wrong.

The map becomes a reference for staff training, process improvement, and identifying gaps before they become complaints. Each phase has pre-filled prompts — replace them with your hotel's reality. Add or remove steps freely.

HOW TO USE
1
Fill in hotel name and date
Each phase tab has a hotel name and date field at the top. Fill these in once — they appear on all printed pages.
2
Work through each phase
Each step has three fields: what happens, who is responsible, and what can go wrong. The prompts guide you — replace them with your actual process.
3
Add or remove steps
Each phase starts with 6 pre-filled steps. Add more with the + button. Remove any that do not apply to your hotel.
4
Download each phase separately
Use DOWNLOAD THIS PAGE ↓ at the bottom of each phase tab to print that phase only. Or use the header PDF button to print whichever tab is active.
EXPORT OPTIONS

Sends a plain text summary of whichever phase you are currently viewing.

PDFPrint — current phase only

Prints whichever phase tab you are on. Use DOWNLOAD THIS PAGE ↓ at the bottom of each tab.

BACKUP ↓Save all phases as JSON

Complete backup of all three phases. Do this when your map is complete.

RESTORE ↑Load from backup

Select a .json backup. Confirms before replacing.

ONE RULE

Map your reality — not an ideal version of your hotel. A guest journey map that reflects how things actually happen is useful. One that describes how you wish things happened is decoration.
